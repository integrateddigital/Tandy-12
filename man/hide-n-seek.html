Game 12: Hide'N Seek

The goal of this game is to guess 3 numbers, in the correct order, which have been randomly selected by the Computer. Only buttons 9 through 12 will be used, but may occur more than once within the chosen sequence.

After you START the game, enter your guess by tapping in a 3-number sequence using buttons 9 through 12. If guess the exact sequence selected by the Computer, you win! But, if you do not guess a single number in the correct order, the Computer will display the correct sequence, and the game will be over, However, if you guess at least 1 number in the correct position, the game will continue, and the Computer will give you the following clues about the correct sequence:
