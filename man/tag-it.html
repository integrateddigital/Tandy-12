Game 5: Tag-It

This game plays much like the classic arcade game Whac-A-Mole. Upon starting the game, random buttons will flash, one-by-one. Your goal is to press the lit button as quickly as possible. If you "whack the mole" quick enough, a short tone will sound, and you will earn a point. The game continues for 110 tries. Upon completing a game, your score will be indicated as follows:
