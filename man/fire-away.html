Game 11: Fire-Away

In this game, buttons 1 through 4 represent attacking invaders, and 9 through 12 represent your missile launchers.

When the game begins, buttons 1 through 4 will begin flashing back and forth. After a short period of time, a tone will sound, and one of the lights in the center column will flash, representing an attacking invader.

The invaders attack in the following patterns (either straight ahead of diagonally):

To destroy the attacker, fire one of your missiles. A flash indicates that you've stopped the enemy's attack, while a flash with a tone indicates that your missile was shot destroyed.
