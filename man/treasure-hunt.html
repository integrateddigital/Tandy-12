Game 9: Treasure Hunt

This game is played similarly to the popular game Mastermind. You must guess, in the correct sequence, the 3 colors which were randomly-selected by the Computer; colors may appear in the sequence more than once. After entering your guess, the Computer will score your attempt as follows:

  • The Computer will play a victory tune if you guess the correct sequence.
  • If 2 positions are correct, buttons 2 and 1 will flash.
  • If only 1 position is correct, button 1 will flash.
  • If no positions in the sequence were correctly guessed, the computer will flash the correct sequence, then play 4 notes.
